# Recruiter Targets

Last updated: 2026-06-30

These are outreach targets from the screenshots, checked against public pages. Use them to complement direct applications, not replace them.

## Best Fit By Lane

### Web3 / Crypto / Blockchain

Proof of Talent: https://proofoftalent.co/

Use for blockchain, Web3, crypto, engineering, product, and go-to-market roles. Best angle: "I am targeting digital asset infrastructure and institutional fintech roles, especially partner engineering / technical consulting / product / GTM."

### Engineering / Product / AI / Security Leadership

Riviera Partners: https://www.rivierapartners.com/

Use for senior product, engineering, AI/ML/data, cybersecurity, IT, and design leadership searches. Best angle: useful if your background supports leadership or senior technical roles; less likely to help with entry-level roles.

### SaaS / Go-To-Market

Betts Recruiting: https://bettsrecruiting.com/

Use for sales, marketing, customer success, account executive, revenue operations, and GTM roles at SaaS and tech companies. Best angle: strategic accounts, customer success, business development, sales, or marketing operations.

### AI / Machine Learning Executive Search

Artemis Consultants: https://artemisconsultants.net/

Use for AI, machine learning, cybersecurity, and executive/senior leadership recruiting. Best angle: only prioritize if your background supports senior AI, security, or leadership positioning.

### Broad Executive Search

Korn Ferry: https://www.kornferry.com/

Use for executive-level searches, leadership roles, and larger-company recruiting. Best angle: not the first stop unless targeting senior leadership, finance, strategy, or executive-track roles.

## Outreach Rule

Send recruiters one sharp lane, not a buffet. Example:

"I am targeting New York fintech and blockchain infrastructure roles where technical delivery, partner integrations, and institutional payments matter. My strongest fit is partner engineering / professional services / technical consulting."

## Candidate Fee Warning

Do not pay a recruiter to submit you to jobs unless you understand exactly what service is being provided. Legitimate contingency recruiters are usually paid by the hiring company, not by the candidate.
