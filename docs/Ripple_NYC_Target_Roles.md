## Recommended First Move

Pick one realistic primary lane and one backup lane. Do not send the same broad resume to all of these jobs.

Best first lane if Antonio is breaking in: customer support, operations, implementation coordination, recruiting coordination, business development representative, compliance operations, or junior analyst roles at Ripple-adjacent companies. Many Ripple roles currently listed are senior, so the path may be through a smaller fintech, crypto vendor, exchange, compliance provider, or payments company first.

Best first lane if Antonio has technical/project experience: technical support, implementation coordinator, junior solutions, QA, or operations automation. Partner Engineering can be a future target, but the resume needs proof before aiming there directly.

Best first lane if Antonio has finance/compliance/operations experience: compliance operations, KYC/AML operations, risk operations, reconciliation, treasury operations assistant, or professional services coordinator.

Best first lane if Antonio has strong software/cloud experience: junior engineering, support engineering, QA, SRE internship/apprentice-style roles, or technical support. Senior/staff Ripple engineering roles need hard technical proof, not just interest in crypto.
